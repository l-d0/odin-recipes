Golden Butter Rice

Description

This golden butter rice, flavored with ginger and turmeric, is a forgotten way of cooking butter rice. It isn't cooked rice with butter stirred in, it really is rice cooked in butter.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/3 cup chopped waluts
  • 1/3 cup sliced green onions

Steps

  1. Melt butter over medium heat and add ginger, turmeric, cayenne pepper, brown sugar and salt as the butter starts to bubble
  2. Add rice and stir in water
  3. As it begins to boil, shake and swirl the pan to settle the rice into an even layer, then reduce heat to medium-low
  4. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes, then turn off heat and let it rest for 10 minutes
  5. Remove the lid and use a fork to fluff and separate the rice grains
  6. Season to taste and garnish with walnuts and green onions
