Banana Bread

Description

This popular banana bread is moist, delicious and packed with banana flavour.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed banana

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease 9x5 inch loaf tin
  2. Combine floue, baking soda and salt in large bowl
  3. Blend brown sugar, butter, eggs and banana
  4. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture
  5. Pour batter into loaf tin and bake for 60 minutes
