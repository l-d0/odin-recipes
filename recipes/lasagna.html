Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but this recipe makes it well worth the wait. This recipe is customisable so you can adapt it, for example, by switching the meat for veggies. Any leftovers can be covered with foil and baked the following day.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 4 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 16 ounces ricotta
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef, onion and garlic over medium heat until well browned
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and water
  3. Season with sugar, parsley, basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and pepper
  4. Simmer, covered, for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally
  5. Cook lasagna sheets in boiling water for 8-10 minutes, then drain and rinse with cold water
  6. Combine ricotta cheese, egg, parsley and salt
  7. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  8. Assemble by layering tomato saunce, pasta sheets and cheese sauce and top with mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  9. Bake for 50-60 minutes
